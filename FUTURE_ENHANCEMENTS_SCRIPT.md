## 💡 TIPS FOR DELIVERY

**DO:**
- ✅ Speak confidently - you know what you're doing
- ✅ Be realistic - don't promise the moon
- ✅ Show you understand priorities
- ✅ Mention specific technologies (Stripe, AI, mobile)
- ✅ Keep it brief - 30-45 seconds max

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Apologize for missing features
- ❌ Say "I didn't have time" (sounds like excuse)
- ❌ List too many features (overwhelming)
- ❌ Sound uncertain or hesitant
- ❌ Spend more than 1 minute on this
